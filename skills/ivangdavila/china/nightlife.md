## City Patterns

- **Shanghai:** high variety from cocktail bars to club scenes.
- **Beijing:** strong hutong bars plus polished districts.
- **Chengdu/Chongqing:** food-nightlife blend with late pacing.
- **Shenzhen/Guangzhou:** modern nightlife corridors with district variability.

## Practical Rules

- Set return transport before final venue.
- Confirm opening patterns by weekday vs weekend.
- Keep one lower-intensity option after heavy transfer days.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Staying only in highest-tourist bar strips.
2. No backup transport late at night.
3. Overstacking nightlife after long logistics days.
