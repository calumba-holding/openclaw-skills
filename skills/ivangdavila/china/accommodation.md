## Booking Strategy

1. Choose fewer bases and stay 2+ nights where possible.
2. Prioritize transit-connected neighborhoods.
3. Book major holiday windows early.

## City Guidance

- **Beijing:** central historic access vs modern business convenience.
- **Shanghai:** FFC/Jing'an for mixed value and movement.
- **Guangzhou/Shenzhen:** pick one anchor district near your daily priorities.
- **Chengdu/Chongqing:** optimize for food zones and evening mobility.

## Property Types

| Style | Best for | Tradeoff |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Hotel | Fast city trips | Prime-zone pricing can spike |
| Apartment hotel | Longer stays/families | Quality consistency varies |
| Boutique | Character-focused stays | Less predictable services |
| Resort | Beach/island trips | Weaker urban flexibility |
