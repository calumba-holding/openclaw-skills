## Route Rules

- Build explicit transfer and acclimatization buffers.
- Keep daytime fuel/food/water planning strict in remote segments.
- Use fewer stops with deeper coverage rather than checklist hopping.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Underestimating route distances and road-time fatigue.
2. Overcommitting to same-day long jumps plus sightseeing.
3. Ignoring weather and wind conditions in exposed areas.
