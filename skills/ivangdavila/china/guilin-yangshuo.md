# Guilin and Yangshuo Complete Guide

## Region Identity

Karst landscapes and river-town routes are the core value. This is a scenery-first region that punishes overpacked schedules.

## Base Strategy

### Guilin
- Better transport entry and practical overnight anchor.

### Yangshuo
- Better for scenic cycling, river viewpoints, and slower countryside pace.

## Route Rules

- Keep one full scenic day with weather flexibility.
- Pair viewpoints by geography to reduce transfer waste.
- Protect one lighter recovery block in humid months.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Compressing Guilin and Yangshuo into a rushed day trip.
2. Overstacking multiple scenic activities without buffer.
3. Ignoring weather visibility before committing to long loops.
