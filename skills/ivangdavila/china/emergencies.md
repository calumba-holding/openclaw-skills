## Emergency Numbers

- **Police:** 110
- **Fire:** 119
- **Ambulance:** 120

## Health and Medical

- Travel insurance is strongly recommended.
- Keep passport copy, medication list, and emergency contacts offline.
- In major cities, international clinics may be available but not universal.

## Transport and Area Safety

- Use planned transport after late nights.
- Keep station and airport transfer plans clear in advance.
- Maintain standard urban awareness in dense transport hubs.

## Weather and Outdoor Safety

- Heat, storms, and air-quality windows can change day plans.
- Carry water, power backup, and offline maps for longer days.
- In mountain or remote corridors, keep extra buffer and supplies.

## When to Escalate

- Call emergency services immediately for urgent danger, injury, fire, or serious incident.
- For non-urgent issues, use local support channels and hotel assistance.
