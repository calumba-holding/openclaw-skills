## Core Rule

Choose hikes by current weather, heat index, and realistic fitness, not only photos.

## Strong Regions

- Blue Mountains and NSW trails
- Tasmania multi-day and day-hike options
- WA and SA coastal and inland routes
- NT and Red Centre walks with strict heat timing

## Safety Basics

- Start earlier in hot periods.
- Carry water, sun protection, and offline maps.
- Respect closures and fire-condition advisories.
- Turn back when conditions degrade.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Underestimating heat and dehydration risk.
2. Late starts in exposed routes.
3. No contingency for fire/weather alerts.
