## Main Wine Regions

| Region | What to Expect |
|--------|----------------|
| Barossa and McLaren Vale | Strong red and food-pairing routes |
| Yarra Valley | Easy from Melbourne with broad cellar-door options |
| Margaret River | Wine plus coast blend, premium day routing |
| Hunter Valley | Popular near Sydney with demand spikes on weekends |
| Tasmania | Cool-climate styles and destination pairing experiences |

## Planning Tips

- Keep tasting-heavy days separate from long drives.
- Book key cellar doors in advance in peak periods.
- Cap tastings and pair with food to maintain route quality.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Overpacking winery stops in one day.
2. No designated-driver strategy.
3. Ignoring transfer times between regions.
