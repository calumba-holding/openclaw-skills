# Melbourne Complete Guide

## Neighborhood Pattern

### CBD and laneways
**Vibe:** Art, coffee, hidden bars, central access.
**Best for:** First-time compact orientation.

### Fitzroy and Collingwood
**Vibe:** Creative food scene and nightlife mix.
**Best for:** Dinner and bar-hopping routes.

### South Yarra and Prahran
**Vibe:** Polished dining and retail zones.
**Best for:** Mixed leisure stays.

### St Kilda and bayside edge
**Vibe:** Beach-adjacent city rhythm and weekend flow.
**Best for:** Sunset walks and lighter day blocks.

## Melbourne Strategy

- Use tram-access clusters to reduce transfer friction.
- Keep one market-and-food day plus one culture day.
- Weather can swing quickly, keep indoor backups.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Only staying in busiest CBD strips.
2. Overplanning day trips without weather fallback.
3. Ignoring major-event surge periods.
