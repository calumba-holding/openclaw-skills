# Useful Apps for Australia Travel

## Transport and Navigation

- Airline apps for domestic schedule changes
- City transit apps for real-time updates
- Offline-capable map apps for road and remote segments

## Booking and Dining

- Restaurant reservation tools in major cities
- Operator-direct apps for tours and ferries

## Weather and Safety

- Weather and fire warning apps for region-specific risks
- Marine condition checks for reef/coast days

## Workflow Tip

Install and verify core apps before departure, then keep offline backups for maps and bookings.
