## Main Travel Regions

| Region | Trip Identity |
|--------|---------------|
| NSW | Harbor city and coast combinations with strong weekend demand |
| VIC | Culture and food depth plus scenic road routes |
| QLD | Tropical north and beach corridors with weather sensitivity |
| SA | Wine-food ecosystems and outback gateways |
| WA | Huge-distance nature routes and premium coast experiences |
| TAS | Cool-climate food and nature with slower travel pace |
| NT | Desert and outback planning with heat-focused logistics |

## Route Planning Rule

Choose one major city anchor plus one contrasting region for shorter trips.
For longer trips, use 2-3 anchors max per 10-14 day block.
