## Social Norms

- Informal communication style with strong practical directness.
- Queue and personal-space expectations are generally clear.
- Respect for local community and land context matters, especially in natural and heritage zones.

## Practical Etiquette

- Follow posted beach, road, and park safety guidance.
- Confirm opening times directly, especially in regional towns.
- Keep driving and alcohol decisions clearly separated.

## Dining and Payment Notes

- Card payments are widely accepted.
- Reservation culture is strong in top city dining zones.
- Tipping is optional in many settings, service-included expectations are common.
