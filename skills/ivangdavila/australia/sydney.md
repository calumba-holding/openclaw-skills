# Sydney Complete Guide

## Neighborhood Pattern

### CBD and Circular Quay
**Vibe:** Landmark density, transit convenience, higher pricing.
**Best for:** First-day orientation and iconic views.

### Surry Hills and Darlinghurst
**Vibe:** Food and cafe density, nightlife access.
**Best for:** Dinner and evening routes beyond tourist strips.

### Newtown and Inner West
**Vibe:** Independent food culture and local pace.
**Best for:** Better value and less tourist pressure.

### Bondi and eastern beaches
**Vibe:** Coastal lifestyle, surf culture, weather-sensitive planning.
**Best for:** Beach and coastal walk blocks.

## Sydney Strategy

- Use landmark zones early, then move to local food neighborhoods.
- Keep beach plans weather and surf-condition aware.
- Reserve top-demand restaurants on Thu-Sun.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Spending every meal near Circular Quay.
2. Overstacking CBD plus beach plus long night in one day.
3. Ignoring weekend transport and crowd shifts.
