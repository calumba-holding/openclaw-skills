# Uluru and Red Centre Complete Guide

## Core Reality

Desert planning is about heat, hydration, and timing discipline. Sunrise/sunset windows usually beat midday blocks.

## Base Strategy

### Yulara and Uluru area
- Best for sunrise/sunset pacing and short guided routes.

### Alice Springs connector
- Better for broader NT route integration.

## Route Rules

- Start early and pause in peak heat periods.
- Keep strict water and sun management.
- Treat long drives as primary activity blocks.

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. Midday-heavy outdoor plans in hot months.
2. Underestimating desert driving fatigue.
3. No contingency for heat and weather warnings.
