## Emergency Numbers

- **Police, Fire, Ambulance:** 000
- **Emergency from mobile (also recognized):** 112

## Health and Medical

- Travel insurance is strongly recommended.
- Keep passport copy, medication list, and emergency contacts offline.
- In remote routes, medical access may require longer transfer time.

## Weather and Outdoor Safety

- Heat, fire conditions, and storms can change plans quickly.
- Carry water, sun protection, and offline navigation backups.
- Follow local warnings for marine, surf, and road conditions.

## Transport and Area Safety

- Plan late-night transport in advance.
- Keep conservative driving plans in remote or wildlife-active corridors.
- Maintain daylight return targets on longer road days.

## When to Escalate

- Call emergency services immediately for urgent danger, injury, fire, or major incident.
- For non-urgent issues, use local health advice lines or on-site support services.
