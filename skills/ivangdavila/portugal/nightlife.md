# Portuguese Nightlife

## The Portuguese Night

Portuguese go out LATE. Dinner at 21:00, drinks at 23:00-midnight, clubs after 2:00. Many places don't get going until 1:00-2:00. Pace yourself.

## Lisbon Nightlife

### Bairro Alto
**Vibe:** Street drinking, bar-hopping, chaotic
**When:** Thursday-Saturday, 23:00-3:00
**Crowd:** Young, local, tourists mixed

**How it works:** Narrow streets fill with people. Buy drinks at bars, drink outside. Police tolerant. Move between bars.

**Best bars:**
| Bar | Style | Address |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Pavilhão Chinês | Eccentric, collectibles | Rua Dom Pedro V 89 |
| Pensão Amor | Former brothel, burlesque | Rua do Alecrim 19 |
| Portas Largas | LGBTQ+ friendly, chill | Rua da Atalaia 105 |
| BA Wine Bar | Portuguese wines | Rua da Rosa 107 |
| Maria Caxuxa | Live music, fado fusion | Rua da Barroca 6-12 |

### Cais do Sodré (Pink Street)
**Vibe:** Clubs, late night, international DJs
**When:** 2:00-6:00
**Crowd:** 25-35, tourists + locals

**Clubs:**
| Club | Music | Cover |
|------|-------|-------|
| Lux Frágil | Electronic, best in city | €10-20 |
| Musicbox | Live music, eclectic | €5-15 |
| Tokyo | Techno, underground | €10-15 |
| Pensão Amor | Also a club, basement | €5-10 |

**Lux Frágil:** Iconic. John Malkovich co-owner. Terrace with river view. Strict door (dress well). Worth it once.

### Santos
**Vibe:** More local, less tourist, alternative
**When:** Late, 1:00-5:00
**Best:** Crew Hassan, Jamaica, Bar da Velha Senhora

### Príncipe Real
**Vibe:** LGBTQ+ scene, upscale bars
**When:** 22:00-2:00 (then move to clubs)

### LX Factory
**Vibe:** Hipster, creative, chill

## Drinks

### What to Order

**Ginjinha** — Sour cherry liqueur, Lisbon specialty
- Where: A Ginjinha bar (Rossio) — standing, quick, €1.50
- Order: "Com" (with cherries) or "sem" (without)

**Vinho Verde** — Light white, slight fizz, cheap
- €3-5 in bars
- Perfect for hot weather

**Imperial/Fino** — Draft beer
- Imperial (Lisbon) = Fino (Porto) = small draft
- €1.50-3

**Poncha** — Madeira's rum punch
- Only authentic in Madeira
- Strong, fruity

**Port & Tonic** — Trendy
- White port + tonic + orange
- Summer aperitivo style

### Prices
- Beer: €2-4
- Wine glass: €3-6
- Cocktails: €8-12
- Ginjinha shot: €1.50-2

## Safety & Tips

### Generally Safe
Lisbon and Porto nightlife is safe. Normal precautions:
- Watch for pickpockets in Bairro Alto (crowds)
- Taxi/Uber late night (safe, cheap)
- Don't leave drinks unattended (universal advice)

### Getting Home
- **Uber/Bolt:** Work until late, €5-15
- **Metro:** Closes at 1:00 (limited night use)
- **Night buses:** Some routes, check Carris (Lisbon)
- **Taxi:** Always available, use meter

### Dress Code
- Bairro Alto: Casual
- Lux Frágil: Dress well (selective door)
- Clubs: Smart casual minimum
- Porto: More casual overall

### ID
Carry ID (passport copy okay). Clubs check at door. Legal drinking age: 18.
