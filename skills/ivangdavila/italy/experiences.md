# Experiences — Italy

Beyond sightseeing: cooking classes, truffle hunting, artisan workshops, and activities worth booking.

## Cooking Classes

### By City

| City | School | Focus | Cost | Duration | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|------|----------|-------|
| Rome | Eat Rome | Pasta, Roman cuisine | €90-150 | 3-4h | Market visit + cooking |
| Rome | Walks of Italy | Various cuisines | €80-120 | 3h | Professional chefs |
| Florence | Desinare | Tuscan cuisine | €110-180 | 4h | Intimate, local home |
| Florence | Cucina Lorenzo de' Medici | Classic Tuscan | €150-250 | 4-5h | Historic setting |
| Bologna | Pasta Grannies | Fresh pasta | €80-100 | 3h | Learn from nonnas |
| Naples | Cooking with Mamma | Pizza, Neapolitan | €70-100 | 3h | Home kitchen |
| Venice | Cook in Venice | Cicchetti, seafood | €120-180 | 4h | Market tour + cooking |

### What to Look For
- **Market visit included** — Shopping with chef is half the fun
- **Small group** — 8 people max for hands-on experience
- **Hands-on** — You should be cooking, not watching
- **Lunch/dinner included** — Eat what you make with wine

## Truffle Hunting

### Where

| Region | Season | Truffle | Best Experience |
|--------|--------|---------|-----------------|
| Piedmont (Alba) | Oct-Dec | White | Most famous, most expensive |
| Umbria (Norcia) | Year-round | Black (summer), White (Oct-Dec) | More affordable |
| Tuscany (San Miniato) | Oct-Dec | White | Combine with wine |
| Le Marche (Acqualagna) | Oct-Dec | White | Less touristy |

### What to Expect
| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Cost | €150-300 per person |
| Duration | 3-5 hours (hunt + meal) |
| Includes | Forest walk with dog, truffle meal |
| Season | White: Oct-Dec. Black: Year-round |
| Book | Weeks ahead in season |

### Specific Operators

| Operator | Location | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Savini Tartufi | San Miniato | Established, includes lunch |
| Urbani Tartufi | Umbria | Famous brand, various experiences |
| Alba Truffle Hunting | Piedmont | Premium, expensive |
