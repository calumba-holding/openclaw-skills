# Legal & Compliance

## ToS Boundaries (What's NOT Allowed)

❌ Automated login or session management
❌ Auto-sending proposals without human review
❌ Scraping job posts or client data
❌ Multiple accounts per person
❌ Payments outside Upwork (direct bank, crypto, PayPal)
❌ Misrepresenting who does the work

## What IS Allowed

✅ AI assistance for drafting (human reviews before sending)
✅ Research and analysis of public job posts
✅ Templates customized for each proposal
✅ CRM tracking outside Upwork
✅ Productivity tools that don't access account

## Consequences of Violations

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Chargebacks >1% | Warning → suspension |
| Off-platform payment | Permanent ban |
| Multiple accounts | All accounts banned |
| Fake reviews | Account terminated |
| ToS circumvention | Funds frozen + ban |

## User Responsibilities

1. **Disclosure**: If client prohibits AI assistance, respect it or decline
2. **Confidentiality**: Never process client NDA data in third-party AI
3. **Accuracy**: Don't claim capabilities you don't have
4. **Jurisdiction**: Client's location determines legal requirements (GDPR if EU client)

## Safe Practices

- Keep all negotiation/agreements in Upwork chat
- Use milestones for payment protection
- Document scope in writing before starting
- Screenshot/save important conversations
- Never share Upwork credentials with anyone
