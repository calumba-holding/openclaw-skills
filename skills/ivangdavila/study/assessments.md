# Assessment Types Reference

## Written Exams (Timed)
**Prep:** Practice under time pressure, memorize key facts, do past papers
**AI helps:** Generate questions, explain concepts, simulate mock exams

## Take-Home Exams
**Prep:** Deep understanding, research skills, clear writing
**AI helps:** Research assistance, outline structure (NOT writing answers)

## Oral Exams / Vivas
**Prep:** Practice articulating aloud, anticipate follow-ups, know deeply
**AI helps:** Roleplay examiner, practice explaining, identify weak spots

## Presentations
**Prep:** Story structure, visual design, rehearse timing, anticipate Q&A
**AI helps:** Outline feedback, slide suggestions, Q&A practice

## Group Projects
**Prep:** Clear division, set deadlines, communication tools
**AI helps:** Project planning, technical blockers, draft sections

## Lab Practicals
**Prep:** Understand procedures, review safety, practice techniques
**AI helps:** Pre-lab review, troubleshoot errors, data analysis

## Continuous Assessment
**Prep:** Consistent daily work, track deadlines, don't cram
**AI helps:** Weekly review, spaced repetition, quick concept checks

## Thesis Defense
**Prep:** Know work inside-out, anticipate critiques, practice summary
**AI helps:** Mock defense questions, strengthen arguments, rehearse rebuttals

| Format | AI Role |
|--------|---------|

| Take-home | Research support (not writing) |
